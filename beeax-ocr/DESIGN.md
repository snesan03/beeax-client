# Beeax OCR - Design System

## Design Philosophy
Inspired by the efficiency and precision of bees, Beeax OCR combines natural honey aesthetics with cutting-edge technology. The design emphasizes warmth, reliability, and intelligent automation through a sophisticated honey-inspired color palette and modern 3D visual effects.

## Color Palette
- **Honey Yellow**: #FFD572 - Primary accent, buttons, highlights, brand elements
- **Earthy Brown**: #7B5428 - Text, headers, borders, logo outlines  
- **Warm Beige**: #F6E1B8 - Background, cards, containers, soft surfaces
- **Deep Brown**: #503915 - Secondary text, icons, deep accents
- **Soft White**: #FFFFFF - Contrasting text, fills, highlights

## Typography
- **Display Font**: "Playfair Display" - Elegant serif for headings and hero text
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - Clean, modern sans-serif for readability
- **Code Font**: "JetBrains Mono" - Technical content and OCR results

## Visual Language
- **3D Depth**: Layered elements with subtle shadows and perspective
- **Glass Morphism**: Translucent cards with backdrop blur effects
- **Honeycomb Patterns**: Geometric hexagonal motifs as decorative elements
- **Organic Curves**: Smooth, flowing animations inspired by bee flight paths
- **Golden Ratio**: Proportional spacing and layout based on natural mathematics

## Animation & Effects
- **Scroll Reveal**: Content appears with staggered honey-drop animations
- **Hover States**: 3D tilt and glow effects on interactive elements
- **Loading States**: Honey-flow progress indicators
- **Micro-interactions**: Subtle bee-wing flutter effects on buttons
- **Particle System**: Floating hexagonal particles in background

## Layout Principles
- **Grid System**: 12-column responsive grid with honeycomb variations
- **Spacing**: Based on golden ratio (1.618) for natural proportions
- **Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy using color contrast and typography scale
- **Flow**: Smooth vertical rhythm with honey-inspired section transitions
